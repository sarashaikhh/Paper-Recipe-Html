Charcoal Sweet Potato Recipe

Ingredients

  • sweet potatoes
  • charcoal grill

Instructions

  1. Light up charcoal grill or campfire
  2. When the charcoal turns white and ashy, its ready.
  3. Place sweet potatoes directly on the hot coals
  4. Roast for about 30-40 minutes, turning occasionally.
  5. They're done when the skin is charred, and they're sc
  6. Carefully remove from the fire, and wait for them to cool
  7. Once you peel, you are ready to eat!
